Got questions

We Got Answers.

Do I need to upload my VOD or stream link? +
No — and that's the whole point. PogClip's Live Engine runs quietly alongside your stream the moment you go live. There is nothing to upload. Your clips are captured and processed in real time, automatically.
How does PogClip know when to clip? +
PogClip watches your live chat in real time. When messages spike — especially hype words like "Pog", "LETS GO", "clip it" — it scores the excitement level and triggers a clip automatically. The more your chat reacts, the more likely a clip gets saved.
Does it work with both Twitch and Kick? +
Yes. Both platforms are supported. You can stream on either and PogClip reads the chat from whichever platform you're live on. Kick support is available on Creator and Pro plans.
What happens to my clips after the stream? +
All clips are automatically uploaded to your secure cloud storage and appear in your PogClip dashboard. They're already formatted for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels. Just pick the ones you want and post them.
Will this slow down my stream or PC? +
Not at all. PogClip's Live Engine is built to be lightweight. It runs as a background process that doesn't compete with your stream for resources. Your stream quality stays exactly the same — PogClip works silently behind the scenes.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your dashboard in two clicks and you won't be charged again. Your clips remain accessible until your storage period ends.
